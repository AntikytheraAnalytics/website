About Antikythera

The name Antikythera (Anti - kith - era) refers to the Antikythera Mechanism, an ancient mechanical device used to predict astronomical movements. The device was built around 200 BCE, and discovered at the beginning if the 20th century, off the coast of the island of Antikythera in Greece.

We take inspiration from this device, as it required meticulous construction, and represented very forward thinking for its time.

Antikythera is an Atlanta based consulting firm with a focus on data analytics and machine learning. Antikythera was founded in 2017 with the goal of developing high performance data architecture solutions, applying machine learning techniques to emerging business problems, and conducting novel machine learning research.
