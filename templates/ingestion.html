Data Ingestion

Your data is central to any business intelligence that you act on. It is therefore crucial that your data can be quickly queried to answer the questions that you want to ask. Here are some of the key considerations that we can help with when it comes to maintaining a high-quality data set.

Cleaning And Filtering

No data is collected perfectly. We can offer our expertise to clean up your data in the following ways:

  • Outlier detection and identification of noise.
  • Mitigating and removing noise.
  • Record deduplication.
  • Making data points uniform.

Schema

Selecting a schema to use for your data may be one of the most important steps when ingesting data. The schema can impact disk space consumption, import performance, query performance, and the level of detail that is retained from the raw data.

We can help select a schema that matches your data and querying needs. Whether your data is kilobytes or petabytes, time-series or graphical, streaming or static, we can engineer a high performance solution.

Redundancy, Availability, and Security

All modern data storage solutions should strive to satisfy the requirements of redundancy, high availability, and security. Redundancy ensures that disk failures, or even natural disasters, do not result in data loss. High availability solutions make your data available at all times, even during major service outages. Security best practices protect your data from theft.

We can offer our experience in cloud architecture design and computer security to offer data storage solutions which meet these important requirements.
